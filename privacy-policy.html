Privacy Policy

HOMestore is an online entity owned by Habib Oil Mils (Pvt.) Ltd. This privacy policy explains how and when HOM can use the information provided by you, who has the right to access the information provided and what rights you have over your personal information. Your use of homstore.pk confirms your acceptance of and agreement to this privacy policy.

Information HOM collects about you:

When you use homestore.pk, we collect and store your personal information to offer a seamless and hassle-free online shopping experience. By registering/signing-up with us, you are agreeing to the collection of your personal data. When you visit our website, place an order, take part in promotions and competitions, contact us in writing through our customer services email and live chat or contact us in verbal through our WhatsApp number, HOM holds your personal information including your name, email address, phone number, home address, shipping/billing address and your credit/debit card details in order to process the order. You credit/debit card details are never shared with third parties and will be only be used for processing your order and collection of payment. We may share your information with authorized and relevant HOMestore personnel such as your telephone number and home address. Such information may be shared with our riders/courier service in order to process your order and to inform you about your order status.

Usage of Information:

The HOMestore may use your personal information for the following purposes:

  • To create your personal account on HOMestore

  • To process your orders (Name, Address, Phone Number, Credit/Debit Card details)

  • To send you notifications through SMS or email about the status of your order/delivery

  • To notify you about the discounts/promotions going on

  • To respond to your feedback and queries and/or complaints if any

  • To inform you about any changes/unavoidable circumstances resulting in the delay of your order

We may also use your information to conduct internal research on our users buying behavior, demographics and interests to help us serve our customers in the best way possible. This will help us improve our products and services according to your needs and optimize your shopping experience at HOMestore by enabling us to display content in line with your needs, interests and preferences.

HOMestore does not share/sell your information to third parties.

Internal Research & Marketing:

We may inform you about our ongoing promotions, discounts and campaigns through email or SMS from time to time. We may also contact you for our internal research purposes which may include (but not limited to) surveys about our online service and its improvement. By making purchase from homstore.pk, you hereby provide your consent to receive pre-recorded messages and on-purpose calls on the phone number and/or email address (in case of direct marketing messages) provided by you at the time of sign-up.

Cookies:

Cookies are small data files sent by our site to your browser and is usually stored on your computer's hard drive or mobile browser. It typically contains two bits of information: a unique ID for each user and a site name. Cookies in this way help us identify you as a unique user every time you visit our website so we can remember you and your preferences and tailor the content of our page accordingly. Cookies also help us remember the necessary information about you such as your username and password so that you don't have to enter the data every time you log-in, in an effort to offer you convenience. These are simply data files and can retrieve only the data they have stored on your device and cannot access any other information about you and your device. Your browser may not accept the cookies but this will restrict the use of our site.
